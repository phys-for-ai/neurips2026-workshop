Call for Paper

We invite submissions at the intersection of physics and AI, including new methods, datasets, benchmarks, position papers, and domain-driven studies that surface open challenges.

Topics of Interest

  • Physics-informed and structure-preserving machine learning
  • Scientific foundation models, generative models, and agents for physical systems
  • Learning for simulation, surrogate modeling, inverse problems, and control
  • AI for materials, fluids, climate, chemistry, astronomy, and high-energy physics
  • Uncertainty, robustness, interpretability, and reproducibility in scientific ML
  • Datasets, benchmarks, and evaluation protocols for physical reasoning

  • Clarity, reproducibility, and empirical grounding
  • Potential to stimulate workshop discussion
